MBA AI Agent Course Optimization Plan

Based on Stanford CS146S (Fall 2025) Curriculum

1. Executive Summary

The current MBA course provides a solid theoretical foundation (LLM basics, RAG, Agent Architecture). The Stanford CS146S curriculum highlights a shift towards practical tooling (MCP, IDEs, Terminal) and lifecycle management (Security, DevOps).

This optimization plan integrates these “Modern Software Developer” practices into our MBA structure, making it more hands-on and industry-aligned for 2026.
